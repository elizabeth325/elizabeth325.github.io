In the beginning Sir Gawain wasn't even Gawain yet. The Welsh authors who first wrote about him named him Gwalchmei and from the start he was considered one of Arthur's greatest knights.

Historia Regum Britainiae
Historia Regum Britainiae is the earliest text to cover the entirety of King Arthur's life. Written by Geoffry of Monmount in 1136, this "History of British King" paved the way for the traditional Arthurian story.
Peredur
Peredur tells the story of a young knight who goes looking for the Holy Grail. In the following centuries this premise would become an entire genre.
